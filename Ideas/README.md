# Ideas

This folder contains various ideas and concepts.


In simple potato terms this sounds way too good to be true on paper. You get space infrastructure, from orbital rings to a space maglev, for a coil gun on the moon and materials that we have currently. It can in theory easily harvest and utilize more energy than global power production. And an even bigger theory.It fulfills tons of fancy terms like active structures, modularity, an all in one package for space that's seemingly cheaper than aircraft carrier. Infrastructure for type 2 and even to Type 3 civilizations. 

First, I'd like to define the idea itself. It's a modification of the Lofstrom loop, but rather than focusing on small particles of mass themselves,  its small individual spheres that act as individual/discrete dynamos, their spin creating alternating current(AC) and gyroscopic. This loop of small alternating Dynamos are then guided by arrays such as Halbach. 

I believe this system could have 3 distinct advantages. 1st the potential for energy into the system. And the usefulness of energy. Because the magnetic fields are able to guide it, it's able to utilize gravity wells for planets like Jupiter, a single orbit Would be able to provide the majority of the energy and velocity. Furthermore. Because the energy is uniform in the system, it means that there's a high activation energy, utilizing large amounts of energy is available inertial confinement fusion, in conjunction with magnetized target fusion means that this system could be one of the only viable systems to provide widespread fusion infrastructure.. Alternating current eliminates the need for complex Transformers. 

Second point is that magnetized Dynamos. Would enable It to be used for multiple utilities, magnetic field created access of protection from radiation, and to breeze themselves. The strong alternating current enables magnetized transport at only the cost of energy. In addition, there are some strong theoretical uses, as an anchor, substituting an orbital ring, and utilizing the magnetic field to dyson arrays, and harvest entire mantels and atmospheres.

A key point is its cost effectiveness, modular expansion. 
Theoretically. Using the magnetic field it would be able to attract and align other magnetic mass such as lunar dust, in doing so it would also be self expanding/bootstrapping itself.
